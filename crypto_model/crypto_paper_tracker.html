Exit types explained
T
TARGET — hit 2R level at any point Saturday or Sunday
Exit price = target from main.py. TradingView take-profit triggered automatically
S
STOP — stopped out
Exit price = stop from main.py. TradingView stop-loss triggered automatically
Ti
TIME — Sunday close, neither target nor stop hit
Most common (78% of backtest trades). Exit at BTC/ETH price Sunday ~9pm ET
